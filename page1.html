The Story Of My Life
India
Junior year of high school, during Covid I had my first seizure.
I woke up in the hospital not knowing what happened and where I was.
I woke up to my mom crying and on the phone with my dad and brother who were also crying,
After that one scarey night, I forgot how to be a normal person again,
The only thing I kept thinking of after that night, was that I won't ever be a normal human bring, andn I will be seen differently by everyone around me.
