Overview

This exhibition features three pieces that show how the reality of life. It will show how we, the audience, do not know what the real truth is, what we think is the truth, end up to be opinions instead of facts. These pieces, Art Thoughtz by Jayson Musson, The Bureau of Inverse Technology, and Transborder Immigrant Tool. These pieces send out the message that people should open their eyes and look at the reality. While these pieces portray the message in their own unique perspectives, they come together to accomplish the same goal as one anther. These pieces show this message through the use of showing facts instead of opinions which do not have any proof to back it up. The audience through this piece will come to an understanding that they should not only rely on opinions, but rather on facts.

Art Thoughtz

By Jayson Musson

This piece was made to show people the styles Musson uses to send his message out. This piece also breaks up Musson's vaeious pieces and breaks down each piece for the audience to understand in a simple way of what his pieces are saying as a whole.

The Bureau of Inverse Technology

By The Bureau Of Inverse Technology

This piece shows a certain investigation the Bureau had done, called BIT Plane. This investigation was done to gather information about the internet as well as the different technologies used in the internet. This piece shows how to never believe what is being told since those are just opinions, instead believe the facts that are in front of you. The facts in this piece the new information and technologies found in the internet.
