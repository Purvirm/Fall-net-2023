The Story Of My Life

The Hospital
Once I got to the hospital, it felt weird, because I have never been taken to a hospital in an ambulance.
Once I got there, they did their routinely tests and forms they need filled out.
After sometime, the doctor comes by and sees me and then tells the nurse to give me the SOS medication as well as adding some Benadryl to it so I can fall asleep,
After awhile I wake up to see that I am being pushed to a room, a private room, since I had tested positive for Covid, and in the back I hear my parents talking to each other,
A few minutes after getting to the room, The nurses start to put wires on my head for an EEG scan.
