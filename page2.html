The Story Of My Life

Coming Back Home
When I came back home, I was fine for a while, until I once again had to go to the hospital.
Except this time conscious, and I was able to see what was happening around me, my parents getting scared, my brother a mess again.
But this time I saw them face to face this time and not on the phone,
Once the police and the ambulance came, it felt real, hearing the sirens as they took me out on the stretcher,
It just didn't feel normal because instead of an ambulance passing my with its sirens for someone else, it was going to pass by several cars because of me.
